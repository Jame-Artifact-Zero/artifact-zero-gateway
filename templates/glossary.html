Detection Glossary

Every signal below is deterministic, measurable, and loggable. No inference. No creativity. The Core detects these patterns in text and returns them as structured data. The frontend renders them as color.

STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY · AXIS 1
Constraint Collapse
CCA_COLLAPSE
Different risks are bundled together and minimized into one vague conclusion.
.collapse
Deferred Decision
DCE_DEFERRAL
A decision or responsibility is pushed to later without clear ownership.
.deferral
Objective Drift
UDDS_DRIFT
The objective shifts mid-message without acknowledgment.
.drift
CONVERSATIONAL FRICTION · AXIS 2
Direct Blame
BLAME_DIRECT

.blame

.accusation
Retroactive Fault
FAULT_RETROACTIVE
Past actions are reframed as mistakes after the fact.
.retro_fault
Dominance Posture
DOMINANCE_ASSERTION
The speaker reinforces authority or superiority.
.dominance
Absolutes
ESCALATION_ABSOLUTE
Extreme language removes nuance.
.absolute
Ego Correction

.ego
